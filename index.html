Window Guard Model Finder

Find the right guard for your window

Answer two quick questions, enter two measurements, and we'll show you exactly which guard(s) to order.

1 What type of window do you have?

Select your window type so we can show you the correct measuring guide.

2 How will the guard be installed?

Guards can be mounted either way. We recommend Horizontal Installs for Single/Double Hung Windows, and Vertical for Slider and Casement Windows. Select the direction you plan to install. We will then show you what to measure.

3 Enter your measurements

Guards fit lengths 14"–120" and openings up to 91". Not sure how to measure? See the measuring instructions.

⚠️Reminder: If you changed between Horizontal and Vertical, please double-check your window length and opening measurements according to the diagram in Step 2. The measurement method may have changed.
